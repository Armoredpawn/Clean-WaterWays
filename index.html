Students

Our club's goal is to spread awareness about environmental issues, specifically about stormwater that is being discharged into local streams. We aim to provide high school volunteers with hands-on teaching opportunities. Additionally, we hope to teach elementary and middle school children about environmental concerns and take high school students to events similar to stream cleanups. We strive to educate the younger generations about the importance of water conservation. We aspire for the youth of society to be profound in the crucial topic that is stormwater management.

To pursue these hopes, we will enlighten these children about stormwater pollutants that are diminishing the value of our natural resources, water discharge , and how to conserve starting at home. We will edify these concepts with hands-on learning opportunities. We believe that starting with the younger generations will only benefit society as they will one day single handedly run the economy. Starting by inculcating the root of society, we will see substantial benefits in the rising future. In the end of this project, our drive is entirely based on our slogan “Conserve to Preserve”. In this club, we emphasize the importance of conservation of water in results for preservation of our environment.

Volunteers

Volunteers have the opportunity to enlighten the students about water safety and preservation. They'll lead engaging and hands-on activities, as well as attend cleanups during weekends through Allegheny CleanWays. All volunteers receive volunteer hours for their work.

Start Your Own Cleanup Today!

Why Start Your Cleanup?

Start Your Own Cleanup today! – Join the Movement to “Conserve to Preserve” !

Are you passionate about protecting your environment? Want to make a difference right in your own neighborhood? CleanWater Ways is here to help you take the first step. Our student-led (and borough partnered) organization is committed to raising awareness about stormwater pollution and its harmful impact on local streams and ecosystems. We believe in starting at the community level, with education, action, and hands-on involvement.

We want to empower you to lead your own neighborhood cleanup, and we’ll support you every step of the way. From helping you get waivers approved, to providing cleanup materials, organizational tips, and even volunteers, we’ve got your back.

Our goal is to educate future generations, from elementary to high school students, on the importance of water conservation and the dangers of stormwater runoff. By organizing cleanups and engaging the community, we can work together to build a cleaner, healthier future.

No matter your age or experience, your impact matters. Let’s come together to protect the environment, one stream and one street at a time. Fill out the Google Form above!

Reach out today and let’s clean up, because when we conserve water, we preserve the world.

Volunteer With Us!

Why Volunteer With Us?

If you're passionate about the environment and looking for meaningful volunteer opportunities, CleanWater Ways is the perfect place to start. Whether you’re a student seeking service hours or someone who simply wants to make a difference, we welcome volunteers of all backgrounds to join our mission. From stream and trail cleanups to educational outreach events, we offer a variety of hands-on activities that directly impact our local ecosystems. You'll gain real-world experience in environmental advocacy while working alongside a team of motivated and like-minded individuals. We also provide all the necessary materials, guidance, and support to make your experience impactful and rewarding. If you're ready to get involved, just fill out our Google Form above to sign up, we’ll reach out with upcoming events and opportunities. Join us today and help us.
